Cryptml 2

Cryptml2 generates secure encryption keys in the form of HTML files. Cryptml 2 the successor to Cryptml.

How to use:

First, download and open your key.html file.

To go the other way, place the setting on "DECRYPT". Use the same offset. Note: character values below 32 and above 126 will be corrupted. To share messages with a friend, there are two good options. The first is to share the specific key you download. The HTML file itself is the key.

The second option is end-to-end encryption. Person A wants to send some message to Person B. They each have a unique key. A encrypts -> B encrypts -> A decrypts -> B decrypts. This requires intermediate sends.
